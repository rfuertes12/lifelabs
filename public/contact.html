Need Help Quitting?

Dial now for a Smoke-Free Environment.

If you are within Metro Manila, you may call DOH's quit line: 165-364.

For Filipinos living outside of Metro Manila, you may send a message to these numbers: 0921-203-9534/0977-627-7539
